Blada - 9 November 2022

The project blada is currently a personal one. The name blada means leaf in Swedish, and most swedes do not put any more meaning to the word than that. Although the word has another meaning for people from Stockholm, used as a slang, meaning to take off. Taking off also entails that one heads to another place, wherever that is, it will definately be ahead of you. Resonating very well with past, current and future ambitions, to always head on.
